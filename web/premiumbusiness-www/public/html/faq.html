Can I keep my domain name?
Yes, you can keep your domain name. You can even transfer it to another registrar such as NameCheap or Google.
What about E-Mail?
You can have as many email addresses as you like. If your domain name is example.com, your email addresses will be something@example.com. You can access your email via an email client such as ThunderBird or a webmail service such as Gmail.
What's included?
Our websites are meant to be informational or offer services. They're not meant for e-commerce. To that end, we offer an appointment/booking system, CMS, forms, and payment processing. We can also include any 3rd party plugin.
What are my responsibilities?
You are responsible for providing content such as text, image, and video.
If you need content created, you can hire freelancers at Fiverr or UpWork.
It is recommended that you provide a logo. The canvas should be transparent.
It is recommended that you provide a favicon. The favicon is the image shown on the browser tab and shortcuts. The favicon is a square image that should look good at 16x16 pixels.
